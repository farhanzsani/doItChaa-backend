Price Estimator

Price your work fairly

Input your product name, size, and complexity. The estimator calculates hours, material costs, and a recommended selling price range in IDR.

  • ✓ Based on real labor cost (Rp15.000/hour)
  • ✓ Size multiplier for fair scaling
  • ✓ Converts estimate into a task automatically
  • ✓ Full estimate history saved per user
